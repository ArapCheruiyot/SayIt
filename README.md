📖 SayIt - Reading Helper for Kids
SayIt is a simple, kid-friendly web app that helps children read independently. When a child gets stuck on a word, they just point their phone at it, tap the word, and hear it pronounced. No typing, no menus – just point and listen.

🎯 The Problem We Solve
Problem	SayIt Solution
Kids get stuck on hard words	Point phone at the word → hear it pronounced
Parents/teachers aren't always around	Kids can get help independently
Typing is hard for young children	No typing required – just point and tap
Reading apps are too complex	One screen, one job – dead simple

🧠 How It Works
text
1. Open app → Camera turns on automatically
2. Place paper under the focus box
3. App detects ALL words in the box
4. Words appear as clickable chips
5. Tap any word → Hear it pronounced
6. Kid repeats it and continues reading
